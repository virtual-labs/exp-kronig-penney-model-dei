### About the Experiment 

This experiment uses the Kronig-Penney model to analyze how a periodic potential in a crystal leads to the formation of allowed and forbidden energy bands for electrons. It demonstrates the origin of energy band structure in solids and explains the difference between conductors, semiconductors, and insulators.
