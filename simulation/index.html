📖 Current Concept

Kronig-Penney Model

The Kronig-Penney model represents a one-dimensional crystal lattice as a series of rectangular potential wells. Each well corresponds to an atom in the lattice.

Schrödinger Equation:
-ℏ²/2m · d²ψ/dx² + V(x)ψ = Eψ

💡 Tip: Try adjusting the potential depth to see how it affects the band gaps!

🔍 Key Observations

1

Energy bands form due to the periodic potential

2

Band gaps appear between allowed energy levels

3

Higher potential depth → Wider band gaps

4

Wave functions are modulated by the lattice

📐 Formula Reference

Bloch's Theorem: ψ_k(x) = e^(ikx) · u_k(x)
Dispersion Relation: cos(ka) = cos(αa) + P·sin(αa)/(αa)
Band Gap Condition: |cos(ka)| > 1 → Forbidden
